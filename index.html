FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from hiring a ghostwriter?+
Ghostwriters write posts. Creator-operators do more: they define your narrative positioning, build a content system, handle multi-platform distribution, and iterate based on what's working. Think of them as a fractional content and distribution partner — not a writer-for-hire.
What platforms do you cover?+
Creator-operators work across LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Instagram, and video formats (Reels, YouTube Shorts, long-form video). Many specialise in specific platforms, and we match based on where your audience lives. Video and Instagram are particularly valuable because they're harder to do well — and that's exactly where strategic operators shine.
How much does it cost?+
Getting matched is free. You pay the creator-operator directly based on the scope you agree together. Typical retainers range from $2,000 to $6,000 per month depending on volume, platforms, and formats. For longer-term strategic partnerships, there are also hybrid structures with reduced fees and equity or rev-share components. Exact deliverables and pricing are always tailored to your needs.
How do you vet creator-operators?+
Every applicant goes through a structured evaluation covering narrative skill, extraction ability, platform fluency, proof of outcomes, niche depth, and professionalism. We don't list everyone who applies — only operators who demonstrate the strategic depth and reliability that founder-level work demands.
What does a first month typically look like?+
It depends on scope, but a typical pilot includes weekly extraction calls to capture your thinking, consistent content output on your chosen platforms, and a strategy review at the end of the month. The exact number of posts, formats, and channels is agreed upfront in a clear scope document.
Who owns the content?+
You do. Content rights, usage terms, and revision limits are all defined in the scope agreement before work begins. We help standardise these terms so there are no awkward conversations later.
What if the match doesn't work out?+
That's why the first month is a focused pilot — clear scope, defined deliverables, no long-term lock-in. If it's not the right fit, we'll help you find a better match. No penalties.
What about the equity / strategic partner option?+
For founders and operators who want deeper alignment, we facilitate hybrid structures where the operator takes a reduced monthly fee combined with equity or revenue share. This works best when both sides are committed to a long-term partnership and the operator is invested in the company's growth. We help structure these deals fairly.
I'm a creator — what's in it for me?+
Higher-value partnerships with founders who value strategy, retainer-based work instead of one-off gigs, and a professional profile that makes you discoverable to the right clients. Plus the option to take equity in companies you believe in. We're building the infrastructure that lets you charge what you're worth.
Is this a marketplace or an agency?+
Neither, exactly. We're a curated matching network — think vetted introductions, not a Fiverr-style marketplace. We vet supply, qualify demand, and facilitate the match. The relationship is direct between founder and operator. Over time, we're building toward a platform that makes this process scalable.
